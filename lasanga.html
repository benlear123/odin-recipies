Italian Lasanga

This is a classic dish that will never be looked over by the eyes of an italian
or anyone that you are trying to impress. A type of dish where if you get right,
you can win over the heart of anyone in an instant. There is something about
creamy pasta sheets with beautifully made bolongese that would make
anyones mouth water. So if yours did, let's dive into how to make it!

By Ben Lear | Made on 6/07/2024

Ingredients

You Will Need:

  • Onion, garlic, carrot and celery, for the flavour base, a soffrito

  • Beef

  • Canned tomato and tomato paste

  • red wine - for extra flavour!

  • Seasonings - beef bouillon cubes (stock cubes), bay leaves, thyme,
    oregano, worcestershire sauce

  • Butter

  • Flour

  • Milk

  • Cheese

  • Lasanga sheets

Directions

Step 1
Make a base with the pasta sheet with the meat sauce, white sauce & bolongese

Step 2
Layer the sheets while repeating the same process with 3 layers

Step 3
Cover the top pasta sheet with white sauce and cheese

Step 4
Pop the lasanga in the oven for 4 hours at a heat of 120C and you
will make your friends and family fall in love with your cooking.
